1) Wait for the containers to finish initialisation
2) Open the browser on port 4000.
3) Click on the "cog" icon.
4) Click "Data sources".
5) "Add data source".
6) Scroll to the bottom and select the YottaDB plugin.
7) Enter "https" for the http/https field.
8) Enter $BACKADD for the address.
9) Enter 443 for the port.
10) Click on the dashboards icon and "New Dashboard".
11) Click Add a new panel.
